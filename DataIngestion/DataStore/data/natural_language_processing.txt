Natural Language Processing

NLP enables computers to understand and generate human language. Core tasks include tokenization, part-of-speech tagging, named entity recognition, sentiment analysis, machine translation, and question answering.

Key technologies: Statistical methods, deep learning, transformer models (BERT, GPT), word embeddings.

Applications: Virtual assistants, content analysis, translation, text-to-speech, chatbots.

Challenges: Ambiguity, context understanding, cultural nuances, multilingual processing.
